Honeypot attacks – what were the most popular hotspots?

In today’s digital world, cyberattacks are constantly evolving, targeting individuals and organizations alike. Honeypots serve as decoy systems to deceive attackers, gather intelligence, and enhance security. By attracting hackers, they help organizations monitor malicious activities, trace attacks, and understand hacker tactics. This guide explores the importance of honeypots in cybersecurity and how they can uncover the sources of attacks.
